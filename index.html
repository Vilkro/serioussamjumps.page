About

What is this?

Serious Sam Classic has a rocket jumping scene that has gone largely undocumented — players have been pushing the limits of what's possible in TFE and TSE for years, producing jumps with a depth that rivals CS:S bhop communities.

This site exists to document that scene: tracking records, breaking down the physics, and teaching anyone who wants to learn. Check out the guide and try your first jump — it's more accessible than it looks.
